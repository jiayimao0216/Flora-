Marketing Campaign Performance

Regional Marketing Campaign Performance Dashboard

Built from Michelin marketing analytics work, this case focuses on turning scattered campaign and regional performance data into a recurring reporting workflow.

Case 01
Business Problem

Regional marketing data was distributed across multiple sources, slowing weekly reporting and making performance gaps harder to identify.

Data

150K+ campaign records, six regions, multiple marketing sources, and 8-10 recurring KPI views.

Analysis

Cleaned fields, validated records, queried SQL datasets, compared regional performance, calculated KPIs, and checked outliers.

Result

Improved reporting turnaround by approximately 30% and helped stakeholders identify trends, gaps, and business opportunities.

People Analytics

Workforce Analytics & Turnover Analysis

From an MBAN capstone, this project converts structured workforce datasets into KPI definitions, turnover risk insights, and stakeholder-ready recommendations.

Case 03
Business Problem

Workforce leaders needed a consistent way to evaluate turnover risk, time-to-fill, and related workforce performance patterns.

Data

Three structured datasets totaling 120K+ rows, standardized fields, documented assumptions, and workforce reporting logic.

Analysis

Integrated datasets, created an analysis-ready table, defined 12 KPIs, and analyzed drivers behind turnover risk and time-to-fill.

Result

Delivered a stakeholder-ready slide deck and written brief with prioritized recommendations and reusable KPI definitions.
