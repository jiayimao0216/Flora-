Work Method

How I approach analysis.

The goal is not to produce more numbers. The goal is to make the next business decision clearer.

1

Start with the business question

I define what the stakeholder needs to decide, which KPIs matter, and what comparison or trend would make the decision easier.

2

Validate the data before analysis

I check source consistency, field definitions, missing values, joins, and assumptions so the final story is built on reliable evidence.

3

Translate findings into action

I turn campaign trends, operational movement, or workforce drivers into concise summaries, scorecards, and recommendations.
